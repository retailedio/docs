<Accordion title="Is the API Cost-Free?">
    Yes, the API can be utilized freely. However, this free plan includes certain limitations. For unlimited access to all features, consider acquiring the paid plan.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Need Assistance?">
    Should you have any queries or encounter any obstacles, feel free to contact us on our [Discord Server](https://discord.com/invite/EUCkqCWrb8). We would be delighted to assist you in answering your questions and guiding you through the process.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Request Counting Mechanism">
    Only successful requests are included in your monthly search count. Searches that yield errors, failures, or are cached, shall not be included.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I Request Additional API Scraping Features?">
    Absolutely! We constantly strive to expand our offering and develop new APIs for a variety of search engines. Should you wish to add a specific feature, submit a request to our Roadmap. Please be aware that we might be unable to develop APIs for particular websites due to various constraints. However, we always consider improvements where feasible.

    [Submit your request here](https://www.retailed.io/business-suite)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What Happens to My Unused Requests Each Month?">
    Please note that your requests get refreshed at the beginning of every month. You can monitor the status of your remaining credits via the dashboard.
</Accordion>
